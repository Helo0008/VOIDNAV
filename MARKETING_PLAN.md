# Orbit Ops Marketing Game Plan (orbitops.space)

## 1. Profiles (To be set up by Bradley)
- **Instagram:** `@OrbitOpsSpace`
- **TikTok:** `@OrbitOps`
- **Bio:** "The interactive 3D lab for orbital mechanics. Master LEO, GEO, and Lagrange points. 🚀 Learn at orbitops.space"

### B. The "Homeschool Hero" (Parent/Educator)
- **Pain Point:** High-end science curricula are expensive and hard to explain.
- **Why OrbitOps:** Professional visuals + gamified quizzes make it an easy "add-on" to a science unit.
- **Where to find them:** Facebook Groups (Homeschooling groups in Australia/USA), Pinterest.

### C. The "KSP Power User" (Gamers)
- **Pain Point:** Kerbal Space Program is fun, but sometimes they want to see "real world" orbital logic without the game's simplified physics.
- **Why OrbitOps:** Side-by-side comparison of 11 real orbit types.
- **Where to find them:** KSP Forums, r/KerbalSpaceProgram, YouTube (comments on Scott Manley or Everyday Astronaut videos).

---

## 2. The "Max" Marketing Tactics (30-Day Blitz)

### Phase 1: The "Free Education" Hook (Organic)
- **X/Twitter:** Tag @EverydayAstronaut or @ScottManley with a 15-second screen recording of the 3D Earth and the Hohmann transfer. People love seeing clean visualizations.
- **Reddit:** Post in r/Space with a "Show and Tell" flair. "I built a 3D lab to help my daughter learn about orbits—ended up building the whole thing out. What do you think?" (People support solo devs more than companies).

### Phase 2: The "B2B" Outreach (Direct)
- **Science Museums:** Email the education leads at the Questacon (Canberra) or the Powerhouse (Sydney). Offer them a free "Premium" account for their staff to show on tablets during tours.
- **Space Startups:** Reach out to Australian space companies (Gilmour, Fleet). Suggest it as a fun onboarding tool for their non-engineering staff.

### Phase 3: The "Wait, what's that?" (Engagement)
- **YouTube Shorts / TikTok:** Post a video with the hook: "Why do satellites fall around Earth instead of into it?" then show the 'Forces' toggle in OrbitOps.

---

## 3. Success Metrics (KPIs)
- **Weekly Traffic:** Aim for 500+ unique visitors.
- **Conversion:** Aim for a 1-2% upgrade rate to Premium ($9.99).
- **Social Shares:** Tracking how many people link to `orbitops.space`.
